Types of stored data

  • E-Mail address of the users

Affected persons

We store the data of the users of our app mainly students of the Hochschule München, exchange students as well as professors using the CiE App/Server.

Purpose of processing

We process your data to provide you with information about the Courses in English offer as well as providing you with the allocated to you.

Duration of the storage

All our data is stored as long as the user uses the CiE services.

Right of objection

You can stop further processing of you data according to Art. 21 DSGVO by contacting the CiE contact person.
